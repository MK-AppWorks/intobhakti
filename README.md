### Into Bhakti

**Into Bhakti** is a Hindu devotional app designed to support your daily spiritual practice.

### Features

* 📿 Digital Jap Counter
* 🙏 Aarti Collection
* 📖 Chalisa & Mantras
* ⭐ Favorites
* 🔔 Daily Prayer Reminders
* 🌙 Clean and easy-to-use interface

Whether you're beginning your day with prayer or ending it with an Aarti, Into Bhakti helps make devotion a part of your daily routine.
